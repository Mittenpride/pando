Flexible Partnership Structures

What a Partnership Can Look Like

Every founder's goals are different, so we offer flexible structures that honor your legacy while unlocking new potential. Here's how it works:

One-time liquidity, without the exit

Get rewarded for what you've built with a competitive, market-rate payout. Take some chips off the table while optionally staying engaged in the business you love.

De-risk your personal balance sheet. Maintain involvement. Keep building what you've started.

Stay in the game, focus on what you do best

Continue driving your business forward while handing off administrative functions (like finance, HR, and IT) to our support team.

You stay focused on what you're great at—your team, your owners, your market. We handle the back-office complexity.

Roll equity, share in the upside

Many founders choose to reinvest a portion of their proceeds back into Pando Collective, becoming "Rolled Equity Partners." This creates an opportunity for future upside.

Own a piece of the entire network. Benefit as the collective grows. Build generational wealth.

Full exit, legacy preserved

For founders ready to step away entirely, we also offer full acquisition paths. We preserve the business continuity, while you move forward knowing your legacy is in trusted hands.

Your team stays intact. Your brand continues. Your reputation endures.
