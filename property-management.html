Frequently Asked Questions

Will I have to rebrand?

No. Your brand equity is valuable, and we preserve it. You keep your name, your visual identity, and your local reputation. The collective supports your brand, it doesn't replace it.

What happens to my team?

Your team stays your team. Your culture stays intact. Your local expertise continues to drive decisions. We provide additional support and resources, not replacement management.

How much control do I maintain?

Partnership structures are flexible and designed around your goals. Whether you want to stay fully involved in day-to-day operations or step back while maintaining ownership, we can accommodate.

What size operators do you work with?

We work with operators ranging from 5 properties to 500+. We look for founders who lead with quality, culture, and vision—excellence at any size. If you meet that bar, you'll get the same high-level support.

How is this different from private equity rollups?

Traditional PE rollups optimize for short-term exits and standardization. We're building something designed to last generations. Over 30% of Pando is owned by founder-partners. This is a high-trust partnership, not a corporate acquisition.
